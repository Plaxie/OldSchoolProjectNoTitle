					HELP(ThreeDGS) : Page 03/03

	* Filled_Cuboid(Length,Width,High,FILLER): 
		This Functions creates a Solid Cuboid with specified Length, Height, width
		and Filler.
		The {Length, Width, High} <int> arguments are used to determine the Dimensions
		of the cuboid. The FILLER <string> argument will be used to 
		fill the faces of the cube.
		
	* Hollow_Cuboid(Length,Width,High,FILLER): 
		This Functions creates a Transparent Cuboid with specified Length, Height, width
		and Filler.
		The {Length, Width, High} <int> arguments are used to determine the Dimensions
		of the cuboid. The FILLER <string> argument will be used to 
		fill the faces of the cube.
