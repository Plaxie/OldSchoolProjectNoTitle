					    HELP(Curves) : Page 01/03
						
	* This module contains Functions that assists in the creation of Curved Structures.
	* There are a few pre-defined print functions in the beginning of this module that print
	  a pre-made shape or structure. These are:
	  
	* up_regular_parabola():
	* down_regular_parabola():
	* left_regular_parabola():
	* right_regular_parabola():
	* circle()
	* up_semi_circle()
	* down_semi_circle()
	
	* Execute them to print their respective shapes.
