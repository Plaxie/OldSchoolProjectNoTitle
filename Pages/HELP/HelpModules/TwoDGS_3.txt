	* Triangle(Xside,_type,FillValue):
		This Function creates a specified type of Triangle with a user-defined
		Length.
		
		The Xside <int> argument is used to determine the Length of a side in the
		generated Triangle.
		
		There are a few Types of Triangles that can be generated, and these are 
		chosen by the _type <string> argument. These are :-
		"tr","dr","tl","dl" and "up".
		
		The FillValue <Boolean> argument is used to Determine
		if the Triangle is Hollow or Filled.
