					HELP(OneDGS) : Page 01/02

	* This module contains Functions that will generate One Dimensional Geometries.
	
	* OneLine(Direction, Length):
		This function creates a Straight line in a specified Direction and Length.
		The Direction <string> argument has six options, These are :-
		
		"HORIZONTAL" , "VERTICAL" , "NW" , "NE" , "SW" and "SE"
		
		The latter are North-South-West-East Directions.
		
		The Length <int> argument is used to determine the length of
		The Straight line
