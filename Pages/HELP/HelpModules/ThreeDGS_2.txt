	* Filled_Cube(Length,FILLER): 
		This Functions creates a Solid Cube with specified Length and Filler.
		
		The Length <int> argument is used to determine the length of one of
		the edges of the cube.The FILLER <string> argument will be used to 
		fill the faces of the cube.
		
	* Hollow_Cube(Length,FILLER): 
		This Functions creates a Transparent Cube with specified Length and Filler.
		
		The Length <int> argument is used to determine the length of one of
		the edges of the cube.The FILLER <string> argument will be used to 
		fill the faces of the cube.
